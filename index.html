Welcome

Project Atlas is an agent-based market simulation developed in Python. The project explores how firms, consumers and macroeconomic conditions interact over time. The industry consists of three smartphone retailers being "Nova", "Vertex" and "Pulse" with each firm implementing their own individual business strategy. Nova is a profit-maximising firm which offers premium pricing and high-quality smartphones whilst maintaining a low total cost level in accordance with their asset-light ethos to minimise liability. Vertex is a revenue-maximising firm which offers a mid-range product and is essentially the best value-for-money smarthphone available within the industry, as they are revenue maximisers, they tend to absorb alot of market share though do carry very high total costs owing to the sheer volume of their output level. Pulse is a slightly more versatile firm, they offer a product to the consumers who carry less of a preference towards the quality/branding of their phone and merely desire the cheapest option in the market, the interesting part about Pulse is that they operate with a very low profit-margin relative to its competitors though still manage to build a sizeable market share due to trends in consumer confidence and income levels often resulting in a portion of consumers becoming available who wish to purchase the cheapest option.
